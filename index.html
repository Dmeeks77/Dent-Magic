Serving West Texas with Excellence Since 1999.

At Dent Magic, we specialize in high-quality Paintless Dent Repair (PDR) for hail damage, minor dents, and door dings. With over 20 years of experience, we've helped thousands of customers across West Texas restore their vehicles — quickly and affordably.

Unlike traditional bodywork, PDR is a non-invasive method that preserves your vehicle’s original paint. Most dents can be repaired the same day, often at up to 50% less than body shop pricing.

We're insurance-approved and honest — if your damage can't be fixed with PDR, we'll refer you to a trusted shop.

Call us Monday–Friday, 9AM–5PM for a free estimate. Or send a photo for an instant quote. Final price is confirmed once we inspect your vehicle in person.
